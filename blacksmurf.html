Black Smurf

Memphis rapper Black Smurf makes dark and moody hip-hop with dense production and twisted samples. In 2015, he released a trio of collaborative Black $uicide EPs with New Orleans duo $uicideboy$. The music blends Southern trap with emo influences. As of 2020, Black Smurf had released five volumes of his Hustle Warfare series with Albany producer Purpdogg. Along with former Raider Klan affiliate Chris Travis, Black Smurf has been linked to the groups East Memphis Boyz and the WaterBoyz collective.

Important Works:

Fully Hydrated - Black Smurf
Fully Hydrated is one of Black Smurf's signature songs. You can hear the Memphis influence through his references to East Memphis, where he's from, his flow and the production. This song from the EP Hustle God is an underground Memphis staple.

Execution 99 - Black Smurf
This track that also come from the Hustle God EP is much more eccentric track, but he continues with the same laid back and slurred vocals that he's known for. The chorus "click clack murda, please don't make me burn ya" sticks in your head every time you listen.
